Title:

Data Science Coding Challenge: Loan Default Prediction |Machine Learning

Rank: better than 70% of my peers

Description:

The goal of this project was to build a classifier that could be used to attribute or not a loan to an individual. What my model does at 87%. However, the data presented a bias which is that the number of people who were refused the loan was more numerous than those who were granted the loan. what should be found in the model. To overcome this, I had to do an oversampling to 35% and a model combination, which allowed me to increase the rate of people who are solvent.

During this project, I used several techniques including: [resampling], [here attempted to rebalance the gap between the two classes], [standardization of data] and [the models combined by voting].

Technos:

Python, scikit-learn, numpy, pandas.
